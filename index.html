Hello there. I'm Matt.

Lately, I've been building and sending emails at MMI. While working with the creative and sales support departments, I have seen the huge impact email can have as a marketing channel.

My experience across all areas of the campaign process has shown me the technical opportunities and importance of data-informed decisions to make an email stand out in the inbox. Creating case studies that measure both the email response rates and retargeting results gave clients the whole story of qualified customer acquisition. Collaborating with local and national advertisers, my list segmentation recommendations have identified high-value prospects. I have worked with ESP vendors and am familiar with large-scale deployments, deliverability troubleshooting, and IP health.

Previously, I did work for startups and small businesses like space519, Azadi, Cloudbot, and Square. In 2013, I organized a TEDx university event.

Currently, I’m looking for a fast-paced company that is eager to experiment with email, avid about data, and wants to build a relationship with their audience. I want to work towards optimizing emails that drive higher returns. I plan to do this by continuing to tap into engaging subject lines/preview text, relevant content, segmented offers, mailing frequency, and landing pages.

Over the last three years as an email marketer, I have lead teams to design and deploy hundreds of email campaigns, created responsive module email templates to decrease development time, and built house lists from scratch that have drove consistent traffic increases. In my free time, I contribute to open source projects by helping monetize an email design inspiration blog and designing startup launch emails.
